What Graphene Can Do

Every feature your server
will ever need.

Twelve full modules, one bot. Completely free for every server, large or small.

🔨
Moderation
Kick, ban, timeout, warn, clear messages, manage roles and nicknames, snipe deleted messages, plus anti-spam and anti-link automod toggleable per server.
⚔️
Levels & XP
Automatic XP gain per message, level-up announcements to a chosen channel, a full server leaderboard, level-based role rewards, and per-role XP multipliers.
🎉
Giveaways
Start giveaways with optional message count requirements, role exclusions, per-role bonus entries, and instant rerolls — fully automated from start to finish.
📨
Invite Tracker
Track who invited who, flag fake invites (accounts under 7 days or quick-leave), award milestone roles automatically, and view a server-wide invite leaderboard.
🤖
AI Assistant
Powered by Google Gemini with live web search. Ask anything conversationally — current events, trivia, recommendations — right inside your Discord server.
🎮
Games
Truth or Dare, Dare, Would You Rather, Never Have I Ever, Paranoia, and a wildcard mode — all with interactive buttons so the game keeps going.
🎫
Tickets & Applications
Private support ticket panels with one-click creation and locking, plus a DM-based staff application system that logs answered questions to your team channel.
🛡️
Verification Gate
Post a persistent verify panel once — members click a single button to receive a role and unlock your server. Survives bot restarts automatically.
⚙️
Server Setup
Welcome messages, DM welcomes, auto-roles, boost alerts, mod logs, Join-to-Create voice channels, auto-response triggers, and reaction role panels up to 10 roles.
🎂
Birthday System
Members save their birthday once and Graphene automatically DMs them a personalised message on the day — zero admin effort needed after setup.
🎲
Fun Commands
Coin flips, dice rolls, magic 8-ball, jokes, inspiring quotes, cinematic quote cards, rock-paper-scissors, love calculator, ship, morse code, fake hacks, and more.
🔧
Utility
Server info, user info, avatar viewer, ping, bot stats, AFK status with mention alerts, snipe, anonymous DMs, and bot-sent public announcements.
